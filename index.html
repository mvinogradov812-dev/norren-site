A memory that thinks
like you do.

01 / Capture

Just speak. It writes itself.

Press the orb. Talk for ten seconds or ten minutes — in English, Russian, or both in the same sentence. Norren transcribes everything and quietly files it away by category.

02 / Understand

It extracts what matters.

Dates. Amounts. Names. Symptoms. Decisions. Claude AI reads each memory the way you would, pulls out what's important, and remembers the context — not just the words.

03 / Recall

Ask anything. Find anything.

"When was my last ultrasound?" "How much on coffee last month?" Norren answers from your own words, with dates, numbers, and context intact.

04 / Reflect

Weekly insights, written by AI.

Every week, a quiet digest of your patterns: where your money went, who you saw, what kept coming up. Not advice. Just clarity.

Privacy

Your memories
are yours.

Stored on your device.

Every memory, transcript, and insight lives on your iPhone. Not in the cloud. Not on our servers. Not in any database we control.

AI without identity.

When Norren queries Claude, it sends transcript text only — never your name, account, or audio. Nothing is linked back to you.

Yours to delete.

Delete a memory and it's gone — from your device, from any cache, immediately. There's no recovery, no backup we can access.

Questions

Things people
ask first.

Is Norren a journaling app?

Not exactly. Journals are for writing about how you feel. Norren is for remembering what happened — appointments, expenses, conversations, ideas — and finding any of it again. Think of it less like a diary and more like a search engine for your own life.

What languages does it understand?

English and Russian, including code-switching mid-sentence. More languages coming.

How do I join the beta?

Apply through the button below. We're letting in the first hundred testers gradually — when you're approved, you'll get a TestFlight invitation in your email.

What does it cost after launch?

Free forever with unlimited memories and 3 AI questions. Premium is $6.99/month or $49.99/year for unlimited AI and Insights. Early supporters keep founding pricing.

Is my voice data private?

Yes. Recordings and transcripts stay on your iPhone. AI features process transcript text only — never your audio, never linked to your identity. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet. Norren is built natively for iOS first. Apply for the beta and let us know if Android matters to you.

Who's behind this?

One developer who wanted a search engine for his own life — and finally built one.
